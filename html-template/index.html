Welcome to BeTreel. We're a social network dedicated to encoraging sustainabilty. Small steps, taken together, can create a large impact. At a random time very day, we'll send out an email with a small, environmentally-friendly task you can do (ex: picking up garbage). Then, you'll post upload a photo of you completing the task for all your friends to see.

To upload your photo, click upload. To sign up for the daily notifications, click sign-up.
